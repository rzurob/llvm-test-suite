There are two kind of TCs non-CAF and CAF (have *f2008caf* in there names). The differences between non-CAF and CAF TCs are: CAF TCs require to be compiled with CAF option, i.e; -qcaf, and non-CAF TCs reject CAF option.


Location of TCs:

Non-CAF:

   F2008/stop/unit_tests/diag

   F2008/stop/unit_tests/func

   F2008/stop/diag

   F2008/stop/func

CAF:

   F2008/coarray/stop/const_expr/unit

   F2008/coarray/error_stop/const_expr/

   F2008/coarray/stop/const_expr/
