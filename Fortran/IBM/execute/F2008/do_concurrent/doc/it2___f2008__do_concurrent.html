Below are the new TCs written for this feature along with an overview of each one.

We are testing nesting up to 3-levels deep for nested do concurrent loops unless otherwise specified. All user-defined procedures are pure (constraint on DO CONCURRENT construct; DO CONCURRENT construct can only act upon pure procedures and can only call pure procedures in the scalar-mask-expr).

We will only be acceptance testing OMP directives because OMP will not do anything for the customer in terms of parallelizing iterations of the DO CONCURRENT loops at this time. For example, the !$OMP PARALLEL directive does not parallelize the iterations of a DO CONCURRENT loop - it executes the loop completely once per thread. Also, OMP does not currently support F2008 and !$OMP DO or !$OMP PARALLEL DO does not accept the DO CONCURRENT construct as an acceptable DO construct for the directive at this time.

Note: Functional test cases are located at $(tsrcdir)F2008/do_concurrent/func/ and diagnostic test cases are located at $(tsrcdir)F2008/do_concurrent/diag/.
